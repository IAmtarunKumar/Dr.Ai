Caution: AI Doctor Chatbot Usage Guidelines

Welcome to our AI Doctor Chatbot! We are thrilled to offer this innovative and convenient tool to assist you with your health-related queries and concerns. However, it's important to understand the following guidelines and cautions when using our AI-powered medical assistant:

  1. Our AI doctor chatbot is designed to provide general health information, suggestions, and support. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  2. If you have a medical emergency or a serious health issue, do not rely solely on the AI chatbot. Seek immediate medical attention from a qualified healthcare professional or call emergency services.
  3. While our AI chatbot is trained on a vast amount of medical knowledge, it may not have the most up-to-date information, and its responses are based on existing data up to September 2021. Medical science evolves, so consult a healthcare professional for the latest guidance.
  4. Avoid sharing sensitive personal health information or medical history with the AI chatbot. Your privacy and data security are important to us. Use our service for general health inquiries only.
  5. Think of our AI chatbot as a supplementary tool to gather information and better understand health topics. Always consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment plans.
  6. AI systems can occasionally make mistakes or misinterpret questions. Double-check any information provided and seek clarification if needed.
  7. Our AI doctor chatbot is intended for use by individuals aged 18 and above. If you are under 18, please consult a parent or guardian for assistance.
  8. Your feedback is valuable to us. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We continuously work to enhance the accuracy and reliability of our AI.

By using our AI Doctor Chatbot, you acknowledge and accept these guidelines and cautions. Remember that your health and well-being are our top priority, and this tool is meant to complement, not replace, professional medical care. If you have any concerns or questions, consult a qualified healthcare provider for personalized assistance.
